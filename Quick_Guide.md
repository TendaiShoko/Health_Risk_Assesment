##  What This System Does

1. **HIV Risk Assessment** (0-100 scale)
   - Analyzes conversations for HIV acquisition risk factors
   - Based on SA NDOH HIV Testing Services Policy 2016
   - Categories: LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH

2. **Mental Health Risk Assessment** (0-100 scale)
   - Screens for depression, anxiety, and severe symptoms
   - Based on SA NDOH Mental Health Policy Framework 2023-2030
   - Categories: MINIMAL, MILD, MODERATE, SEVERE

3. **Treatment Recommendations**
   - Evidence-based plans aligned with SA NDOH guidelines
   - Urgency determination
   - Specific referral pathways
   - Integrated care for comorbidities
